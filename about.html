About Sunrise Bakery

Baking with passion since 2005

Our Story

Sunrise Bakery was born from a simple dream: to bring the warmth and comfort of traditional European baking to our local community. In 2005, master baker Maria Chen opened our doors with just a small oven, a handful of recipes passed down through generations, and a commitment to quality that would define everything we do.

Twenty years later, that commitment hasn't changed. Every morning before dawn, our team of dedicated bakers arrives to begin the day's work. We still mix our dough by hand, proof our breads with patience, and bake everything fresh daily. We believe that the best baked goods come from quality ingredients, time-honored techniques, and genuine care.

Today, Sunrise Bakery has become more than just a bakery – it's a gathering place where neighbors become friends over morning coffee and fresh croissants, where birthday cakes become cherished memories, and where the simple act of breaking bread brings people together.

What We Believe
